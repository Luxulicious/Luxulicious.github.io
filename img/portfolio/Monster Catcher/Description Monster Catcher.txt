Description
A game where you have to catch wandering monsters over various mobile phone screens.
Made with co-students Malyx (Design/graphics) & Yorrin (UI/graphic behavioural programming)

Challenges and achievements
Setting up a strong foundation in an earlier prototype that could 
be build upon with network functionality.
Creating a smooth online experience, first experimenting with a Node.JS server.
Resolving time-consuming issues for designers by adding in inspector functionality.
