Description
A pseudo rythm game where you have to avoid getting hit by the 
recordneedle.
Made during 48 hour gamejam.
Programming & design done by Tom Ruijs
Recorddisc graphics done by Max Claus
Remaining graphics done by Tom Ruijs

Challenges and achievements
The hardest part of making the game was getting the centrifugal 
nature of the mechanics to work.
Ensuring physics, collisions and movement keep behaving correctly 
while dealing with several "centrifugal" forces.
